Company Overview

Strategic Workforce & Business Support Partner

Talent Core HR Solutions is a global-focused consultancy specializing in recruitment, HR support, PRO services, and business setup coordination. We work closely with companies to streamline hiring, simplify administrative processes, and build structured operational frameworks that support long-term growth.

With a strong understanding of global regulations and business dynamics, we bridge the gap between companies and reliable service execution — ensuring efficiency, compliance, and consistency at every stage.

Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, structured, and efficient workforce and business support solutions that empower organizations to grow with confidence. We are committed to simplifying HR processes, enhancing operational efficiency, and building strong, people-centric systems that drive long-term success through innovation, integrity, and a deep understanding of business needs.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name globally for integrated recruitment and business services, recognized for precision, speed, and commitment to long-term value creation. We envision building a future where businesses rely on us as a strategic partner for sustainable growth, workforce excellence, and operational success.

Our Services

End-to-end HR and business solutions tailored for global growth

👥

Recruitment

Our recruitment services deliver fast, reliable, and high-quality hiring solutions across the UAE and globally. We manage local hiring, coordinate overseas talent sourcing, and handle bulk recruitment with structured processes.

"Empowering businesses with swift, strategic talent solutions — equipping companies with efficient HR strategies that drive competitive growth."
  • Fast, reliable, and high-quality hiring solutions
  • Management of local hiring requirements
  • Coordination of overseas talent sourcing
  • Handling of bulk recruitment with structured processes
  • Detailed candidate screening and evaluation
  • Ensuring candidates meet role-specific requirements
  • Reducing hiring risks for businesses
  • Building strong and capable teams
📋

HR Support

We provide structured HR support services that help companies maintain organized and compliant internal operations. From policy development to workforce management, we ensure clarity, consistency, and efficiency.

"By implementing clear systems and processes, we enable businesses to improve workforce management, reduce internal challenges, and maintain a professional environment."
  • HR advisory and strategic consulting
  • Documentation management and record-keeping
  • Internal HR audits and compliance reviews
  • Employee grievance handling and mediation
  • Policy development and implementation
  • Workforce management frameworks
  • HR process optimization
  • Performance management support
🏛️

PRO Services

Our PRO services simplify complex government-related processes through a reliable and partner-based approach. We support visa processing, licensing, and regulatory procedures required to operate in the UAE.

"By coordinating with trusted partners, we ensure accuracy, efficiency, and timely execution — allowing businesses to focus on operations without administrative delays."
  • Visa processing and renewal services
  • Business licensing and registration
  • Trade license renewal
  • Document attestation and notarization
  • Emirates ID application and renewal
  • Labor and immigration card processing
  • Work permit applications and renewals
  • Government liaison and representation
  • VAT registration and compliance
  • Health insurance registration and renewal
  • Payroll management and processing
  • Trademark registration and protection
  • Company liquidation assistance
  • Translation and certification services

Business Setup

Talent Core provides structured guidance for businesses looking to establish or expand in the UAE. We assist with setup planning, business structuring, and coordination with relevant authorities and partners.

"Our approach ensures that every step is handled efficiently and correctly — reducing risks, saving time, and enabling a smooth market entry or expansion process."
  • Company formation (mainland & free zone)
  • Business structuring and setup planning
  • Expert advice on free zone vs mainland options
  • Comprehensive support for licensing and registration
  • Company name registration and amendment
  • Financial consulting and legal compliance assistance
  • Setting up offshore companies
  • Sponsorship and corporate nominee services
  • End-to-end solutions including PRO services
  • Strategic advice for global establishment
